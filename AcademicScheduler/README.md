# Academic Scheduler
## Personal Timetable and Task Management System

**Purpose**
The purpose of the software is to make keeping up with everyday tasks and lessons more convenient. With **daily reminders** of deadlines and **personalized to-do lists**, users can keep every feature in one place to organize and visualize their plans clearly.

**Target Audience**
This product is designed for **IFE students** at the Technical University of Lodz. 
* *Requirement:* Must have an active student account.
* *Intended Use:* Everyday academic management.
